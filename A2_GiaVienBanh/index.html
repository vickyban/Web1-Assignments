Moon

The Moon and the Earth are held together by gravity. The Earth is much more massive than the Moon causing the Moon to orbit the Earth. The Moon revolves (orbits) eastward looking in the sky from Earth. Each orbit takes 27.3 days. The Moon also rotates, or spins on an internal axis once every 27.3 days.The Earth/Moon system also revolves around the Sun, taking 365.25 days (or a year) to complete one orbit.
